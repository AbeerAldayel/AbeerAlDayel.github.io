Research scope and other activities

Please refer to publications page for most recent studies. My research interests lie primarily in Computational Social Science and Natural Language Processing . Most generally, I am working on NLP models and computational methods to recognize social aspects of language and modeling the use of language in social contexts to understand different phenomena with focus on multilingual/social processing, such as tackling biases, abusive language, and misinformation.

- I am not interested in monolingual or polyglossia/dialects, aka. Arabic (NLP/ Social Network) or Healthcare/ Applied Educational/ Privacy or Cyber-security applications!

- I am not interested with startup ''pure business models'' or applications

Projects/Supervision and mentoring
