I am a PhD student at University of Edinburgh, in ILCC supervised by Walid Magdy and Bonnie Webber . I am member of Social Media Analysis and Support for Hummanities (SMASH) research group. My area of research is Computational Social Science, where I study stance detection and how the stance is being modeled in Social Media, with focus on the online social behavior.

I earned a BSc and MSc in Computer and Information Sciences from King Saud University. I continue my career in academia as a lecturer in College of Computer and Information Sciences, the Information Technology Department at King Saud University.
